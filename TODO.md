# Major Redesign Change

I would like to redesign the app so that the chat is prioritized, and we instead have configuration (voices/moderation/users pages) moved into a settings popup. The popup should still have a sidebar-based navigation system where the various different categories of settings are distinguished.

The logo should be moved to the header, and on the right side of the header we should have the status indicator and settings button. This will make it easier to prioritize the app itself and have settings be a dedicated but still easy to reach button.
